17

1 spake unto Moses saying 2 Speak unto the children of Israel and take of every one of them a rod according to the house fathers of all their princes according to the house of their fathers twelve rods write thou every man' name upon his rod 3 And thou shalt write Aaron' name upon the rod of Levi for one rod for the head of the house of their fathers 4 And thou shalt lay them up in the tabernacle of the congregation before the testimony where I will meet 5 the man' rod whom I shall choose shall blossom and I will make to cease from me the murmurings of the children of Israel whereby they murmur 6 And Moses spake unto the children of Israel and every one of their princes gave him a rod apiece, for each prince one according to their fathers houses twelve rods and the rod of Aaron among their rods 7 And Moses laid up the rods before in the tabernacle of witness 8 And it came to pass, that on the morrow Moses went into the tabernacle of witness and, behold, the rod of Aaron for the house of Levi was budded and brought forth buds and bloomed blossoms and yielded almonds 9 And Moses brought out all the rods from before unto all the children of Israel and they looked and took every man his rod 10 said unto Moses Bring Aaron' rod again before the testimony to be kept for a token against the rebels and thou shalt quite take away their murmurings from me, that they die 11 And Moses did commanded him, so did 12 And the children of Israel spake unto Moses saying Behold, we die we perish we all perish 13 Whosoever cometh any thing near unto the tabernacle shall die shall we be consumed with dying

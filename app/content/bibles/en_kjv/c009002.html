1 And Hannah prayed and said My heart rejoiceth mine horn is exalted my mouth is enlarged over mine enemies because I rejoice in thy salvation 2 none holy none beside any rock like our God 3 Talk no more so exceeding proudly arrogancy come out of your mouth a God of knowledge and by him actions are weighed 4 The bows of the mighty men broken and they that stumbled are girded with strength 5 full have hired out themselves for bread hungry ceased so that the barren hath born seven and she that hath many children is waxed feeble 6 killeth and maketh alive he bringeth down to the grave and bringeth up 7 maketh poor and maketh rich he bringeth low and lifteth up 8 He raiseth up the poor out of the dust lifteth up the beggar from the dunghill to set among princes and to make them inherit the throne of glory for the pillars of the earth and he hath set the world 9 He will keep the feet of his saints and the wicked shall be silent in darkness for by strength shall no man prevail 10 The adversaries shall be broken to pieces out of heaven shall he thunder shall judge the ends of the earth and he shall give strength unto his king and exalt the horn of his anointed 11 And Elkanah went to Ramah to his house And the child did minister before Eli the priest 12 Now the sons of Eli sons of Belial they knew 13 And the priests custom with the people when any man offered sacrifice the priest' servant came while the flesh was in seething with a fleshhook of three teeth in his hand 14 And he struck into the pan or kettle or caldron or pot all that the fleshhook brought up the priest took for himself. So they did in Shiloh unto all the Israelites that came 15 Also before they burnt the fat the priest' servant came and said to the man that sacrificed Give flesh to roast for the priest for he will not have sodden flesh of thee, but raw 16 any man said unto him, Let them not fail to burn the fat presently take as thy soul desireth then he would answer but thou shalt give now: and if not, I will take by force 17 Wherefore the sin of the young men was very great before for men abhorred the offering 18 But Samuel ministered before a child girded with a linen ephod 19 Moreover his mother made him a little coat and brought to him from year to year when she came up with her husband to offer the yearly sacrifice 20 And Eli blessed Elkanah and his wife and said give thee seed of this woman for the loan which is lent And they went unto their own home 21 And visited Hannah so that she conceived and bare three sons and two daughters And the child Samuel grew before 22 Now Eli was very old and heard all that his sons did unto all Israel and how they lay with the women that assembled the door of the tabernacle of the congregation 23 And he said unto them, Why do ye such things for I hear of your evil dealings by all this people 24 Nay, my sons no good report that I hear people to transgress 25 If one man sin against another the judge shall judge him: but if a man sin who shall intreat for him? Notwithstanding they hearkened not unto the voice of their father would slay 26 And the child Samuel grew on and was in favour and also with men 27 And there came a man of God unto Eli and said unto him, Thus saith Did I plainly appear unto the house of thy father when they were in Egypt in Pharaoh' house 28 And did I choose him out of all the tribes of Israel my priest to offer upon mine altar to burn incense to wear an ephod before me? and did I give unto the house of thy father all the offerings made by fire of the children of Israel 29 Wherefore kick ye at my sacrifice and at mine offering which I have commanded habitation and honourest thy sons above me, to make yourselves fat with the chiefest of all the offerings of Israel my people 30 God of Israel saith I said indeed thy house and the house of thy father should walk before me for ever saith Be it far from me for them that honour me I will honour and they that despise me shall be lightly esteemed 31 Behold, the days come that I will cut off thine arm and the arm of thy father' house that there shall not be an old man in thine house 32 And thou shalt see an enemy habitation shall give Israel and there shall not be an old man in thine house for ever 33 And the man I shall not cut off from mine altar to consume thine eyes and to grieve thine heart and all the increase of thine house shall die in the flower of their age 34 a sign unto thee, that shall come upon thy two sons on Hophni and Phinehas in one day they shall die both 35 And I will raise me up a faithful priest shall do in mine heart and in my mind and I will build him a sure house and he shall walk before mine anointed for ever 36 every one that is left in thine house shall come crouch to him for a piece of silver and a morsel of bread and shall say Put me, I pray thee, into one of the priests'offices that I may eat a piece of bread

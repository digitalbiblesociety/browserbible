2Chr 25

24

1 Joash seven years old when he began to reign and he reigned forty years in Jerusalem His mother' name Zibiah of Beersheba 2 And Joash did right in the sight all the days of Jehoiada the priest 3 And Jehoiada took for him two wives and he begat sons and daughters 4 And it came to pass after Joash was minded to repair the house 5 And he gathered together the priests and the Levites and said to them, Go out unto the cities of Judah and gather of all Israel money to repair the house of your God from year to year and see that ye hasten the matter Howbeit the Levites hastened 6 And the king called for Jehoiada the chief and said unto him, Why hast thou not required of the Levites to bring in out of Judah and out of Jerusalem the collection of Moses the servant and of the congregation of Israel for the tabernacle of witness 7 For the sons of Athaliah that wicked woman, had broken up the house of God and also all the dedicated things of the house did they bestow upon Baalim 8 And at the king' commandment they made a chest and set it without at the gate of the house 9 And they made a proclamation through Judah and Jerusalem to bring in the collection Moses the servant of God upon Israel in the wilderness 10 And all the princes and all the people rejoiced and brought in and cast into the chest until they had made an end 11 Now it came to pass, that at what time the chest was brought unto the king' office by the hand of the Levites and when they saw much money the king' scribe and the high priest' officer came and emptied the chest and took it, and carried it to his place again Thus they did day by day and gathered money in abundance 12 And the king and Jehoiada gave it to such as did the work of the service of the house and hired masons and carpenters to repair the house and also such as wrought iron and brass to mend the house 13 So the workmen wrought and the work was perfected by them and they set the house of God in his state and strengthened 14 And when they had finished they brought the rest of the money before the king and Jehoiada whereof were made vessels for the house vessels to minister and to offer and spoons and vessels of gold and silver And they offered burnt offerings in the house continually all the days of Jehoiada 15 But Jehoiada waxed old and was full of days when he died an hundred and thirty years old when he died 16 And they buried him in the city of David among the kings because he had done good in Israel both toward God and toward his house 17 Now after the death of Jehoiada came the princes of Judah and made obeisance to the king Then the king hearkened 18 And they left the house God of their fathers and served groves and idols and wrath came upon Judah and Jerusalem for this their trespass 19 Yet he sent prophets to them, to bring them again and they testified against them: but they would not give ear 20 And the Spirit of God came upon Zechariah the son of Jehoiada the priest which stood above the people and said unto them, Thus saith God Why transgress ye the commandments that ye cannot prosper because ye have forsaken he hath also forsaken 21 And they conspired against him, and stoned him with stones at the commandment of the king in the court of the house 22 Thus Joash the king remembered not the kindness which Jehoiada his father had done to him, but slew his son And when he died he said look and require 23 And it came to pass at the end of the year the host of Syria came up against him: and they came to Judah and Jerusalem and destroyed all the princes of the people from among the people and sent all the spoil of them unto the king of Damascus 24 For the army of the Syrians came with a small company of men delivered a very great host into their hand because they had forsaken God of their fathers So they executed judgment against Joash 25 And when they were departed from him, (for they left him in great diseases his own servants conspired against him for the blood of the sons of Jehoiada the priest and slew him on his bed and he died and they buried him in the city of David but they buried him not in the sepulchres of the kings 26 And these are they that conspired against him; Zabad the son of Shimeath an Ammonitess and Jehozabad the son of Shimrith a Moabitess 27 his sons and the greatness of the burdens upon him, and the repairing of the house of God written in the story of the book of the kings And Amaziah his son reigned

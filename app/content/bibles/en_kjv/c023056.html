56

1 Thus saith Keep ye judgment and do justice for my salvation near to come and my righteousness to be revealed 2 Blessed the man doeth this, and the son of man layeth hold on it; that keepeth the sabbath from polluting it, and keepeth his hand from doing any evil 3 Neither let the son of the stranger that hath joined speak saying hath utterly separated me from his people neither let the eunuch say a dry tree 4 For thus saith unto the eunuchs that keep my sabbaths and choose that please me, and take hold of my covenant 5 Even unto them will I give in mine house and within my walls a place and a name better than of sons and of daughters I will give them an everlasting name that shall not be cut off 6 Also the sons of the stranger that join to serve him, and to love the name to be his servants every one that keepeth the sabbath from polluting it, and taketh hold of my covenant 7 Even them will I bring to my holy mountain and make them joyful in my house of prayer their burnt offerings and their sacrifices accepted upon mine altar for mine house shall be called an house of prayer for all people 8 The Lord GOD which gathereth the outcasts of Israel saith Yet will I gather to him, beside those that are gathered 9 All ye beasts of the field come to devour all ye beasts in the forest 10 His watchmen blind they are all ignorant all dumb dogs they cannot bark sleeping lying down loving to slumber 11 greedy dogs can never have enough shepherds cannot understand they all look to their own way every one for his gain from his quarter 12 Come I will fetch wine and we will fill ourselves with strong drink and to morrow shall be as this day much more abundant

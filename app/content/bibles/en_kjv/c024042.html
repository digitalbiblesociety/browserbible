Jer 43

42

1 Then all the captains of the forces and Johanan the son of Kareah and Jezaniah the son of Hoshaiah and all the people from the least even unto the greatest came near 2 And said unto Jeremiah the prophet Let, we beseech thee, our supplication be accepted before thee, and pray thy God for all this remnant for we are left a few of many as thine eyes do behold 3 thy God may shew us the way wherein we may walk and the thing that we may do 4 Then Jeremiah the prophet said unto them, I have heard behold, I will pray your God according to your words whatsoever thing shall answer you, I will declare unto you; I will keep nothing back 5 Then they said to Jeremiah be a true and faithful witness between us, if we do not even according to all things thy God shall send 6 good evil we will obey the voice our God to whom we send thee; that it may be well with us, when we obey the voice our God 7 And it came to pass after ten days that the word came unto Jeremiah 8 Then called he Johanan the son of Kareah and all the captains of the forces with him, and all the people from the least even to the greatest 9 And said unto them, Thus saith the God of Israel unto whom ye sent me to present your supplication before 10 If ye will still abide in this land then will I build down and I will plant up for I repent me of the evil that I have done 11 Be not afraid of the king of Babylon of whom ye are afraid be not afraid of him, saith with you to save you, and to deliver you from his hand 12 And I will shew mercies unto you, that he may have mercy upon you, and cause you to return to your own land 13 But if ye say We will not dwell in this land neither obey the voice your God 14 Saying No; but we will go into the land of Egypt where we shall see no war nor hear the sound of the trumpet nor have hunger of bread and there will we dwell 15 And now therefore hear the word ye remnant of Judah Thus saith of hosts the God of Israel If ye wholly set your faces to enter into Egypt and go to sojourn 16 the sword which ye feared shall overtake you there in the land of Egypt and the famine whereof ye were afraid shall follow close after you there in Egypt and there ye shall die 17 So shall it be with all the men that set their faces to go into Egypt to sojourn there; they shall die by the sword by the famine and by the pestilence and none of them shall remain or escape from the evil that I will bring 18 For thus saith of hosts the God of Israel As mine anger and my fury hath been poured forth upon the inhabitants of Jerusalem so shall my fury be poured forth upon you, when ye shall enter into Egypt and ye shall be an execration and an astonishment and a curse and a reproach and ye shall see this place 19 hath said concerning you, O ye remnant of Judah Go ye not into Egypt know certainly that I have admonished you this day 20 For ye dissembled in your hearts when ye sent your God saying Pray our God our God shall say so declare unto us, and we will do 21 I have this day declared to you; but ye have not obeyed the voice your God for the which he hath sent 22 Now therefore know certainly that ye shall die by the sword by the famine and by the pestilence in the place whither ye desire to go to sojourn

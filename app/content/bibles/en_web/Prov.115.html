Ecclesiastes 1

115

1 Not to us, Yahweh, not to us, 2 Why should the nations say, 3 But our God is in the heavens. 4 Their idols are silver and gold, 5 They have mouths, but they don’t speak. 6 They have ears, but they don’t hear. 7 They have hands, but they don’t feel. 8 Those who make them will be like them; 9 Israel, trust in Yahweh! 10 House of Aaron, trust in Yahweh! 11 You who fear Yahweh, trust in Yahweh! 12 Yahweh remembers us. He will bless us. 13 He will bless those who fear Yahweh, 14 May Yahweh increase you more and more, 15 Blessed are you by Yahweh, 16 The heavens are the heavens of Yahweh; 17 The dead don’t praise Yah, 18 But we will bless Yah,

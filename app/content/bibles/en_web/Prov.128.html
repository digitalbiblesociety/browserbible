Ecclesiastes 1

128

1 Blessed is everyone who fears Yahweh, 2 For you will eat the labor of your hands. 3 Your wife will be as a fruitful vine, 4 Behold, thus is the man blessed who fears Yahweh. 5 May Yahweh bless you out of Zion, 6 Yes, may you see your children’s children.

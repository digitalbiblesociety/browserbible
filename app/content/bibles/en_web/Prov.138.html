Ecclesiastes 1

138

1 I will give you thanks with my whole heart. 2 I will bow down toward your holy temple, 3 In the day that I called, you answered me. 4 All the kings of the earth will give you thanks, Yahweh, 5 Yes, they will sing of the ways of Yahweh; 6 For though Yahweh is high, yet he looks after the lowly; 7 Though I walk in the midst of trouble, you will revive me. 8 Yahweh will fulfill that which concerns me;

Proverbs 46 (WEB)

Ecclesiastes 1

46

1 God is our refuge and strength, 2 Therefore we won’t be afraid, though the earth changes, 3 though its waters roar and are troubled, 4 There is a river, the streams of which make the city of God glad, 5 God is in her midst. She shall not be moved. 6 The nations raged. The kingdoms were moved. 7 Yahweh of Armies is with us. 8 Come, see Yahweh’s works, 9 He makes wars cease to the end of the earth. 10 “Be still, and know that I am God. 11 Yahweh of Armies is with us.

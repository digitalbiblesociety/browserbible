Ecclesiastes 1

54

1 Save me, God, by your name. 2 Hear my prayer, God. 3 For strangers have risen up against me. 4 Behold, God is my helper. 5 He will repay the evil to my enemies. 6 With a free will offering, I will sacrifice to you. 7 For he has delivered me out of all trouble.

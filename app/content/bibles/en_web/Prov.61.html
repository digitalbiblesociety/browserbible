Ecclesiastes 1

61

1 Hear my cry, God. 2 From the end of the earth, I will call to you, when my heart is overwhelmed. 3 For you have been a refuge for me, 4 I will dwell in your tent forever. 5 For you, God, have heard my vows. 6 You will prolong the king’s life; 7 He shall be enthroned in God’s presence forever. 8 So I will sing praise to your name forever,

Ecclesiastes 1

67

1 May God be merciful to us, bless us, 2 That your way may be known on earth, 3 let the peoples praise you, God. 4 Oh let the nations be glad and sing for joy, 5 Let the peoples praise you, God. 6 The earth has yielded its increase. 7 God will bless us.

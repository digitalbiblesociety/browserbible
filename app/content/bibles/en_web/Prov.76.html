Proverbs 76 (WEB)

Ecclesiastes 1

76

1 In Judah, God is known. 2 His tabernacle is also in Salem; 3 There he broke the flaming arrows of the bow, 4 Glorious are you, and excellent, 5 Valiant men lie plundered, 6 At your rebuke, God of Jacob, 7 You, even you, are to be feared. 8 You pronounced judgment from heaven. 9 when God arose to judgment, 10 Surely the wrath of man praises you. 11 Make vows to Yahweh your God, and fulfill them! 12 He will cut off the spirit of princes.

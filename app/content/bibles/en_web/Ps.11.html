11

1 In Yahweh, I take refuge. 2 For, behold, the wicked bend their bows. 3 If the foundations are destroyed, 4 Yahweh is in his holy temple. 5 Yahweh examines the righteous, 6 On the wicked he will rain blazing coals; 7 For Yahweh is righteous.

1 I was glad when they said to me, 2 Our feet are standing within your gates, Jerusalem; 3 Jerusalem, that is built as a city that is compact together; 4 where the tribes go up, even Yah’s tribes, 5 For there are set thrones for judgment, 6 Pray for the peace of Jerusalem. 7 Peace be within your walls, 8 For my brothers’ and companions’ sakes, 9 For the sake of the house of Yahweh our God,

Psalm 131

130

1 Out of the depths I have cried to you, Yahweh. 2 Lord, hear my voice. 3 If you, Yah, kept a record of sins, 4 But there is forgiveness with you, 5 I wait for Yahweh. 6 My soul longs for the Lord more than watchmen long for the morning; 7 Israel, hope in Yahweh, 8 He will redeem Israel from all their sins.

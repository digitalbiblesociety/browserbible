Psalm 138

137

1 By the rivers of Babylon, there we sat down. 2 On the willows in its midst, 3 For there, those who led us captive asked us for songs. 4 How can we sing Yahweh’s song in a foreign land? 5 If I forget you, Jerusalem, 6 Let my tongue stick to the roof of my mouth if I don’t remember you; 7 Remember, Yahweh, against the children of Edom, 8 Daughter of Babylon, doomed to destruction, 9 Happy shall he be,

143

1 Hear my prayer, Yahweh. 2 Don’t enter into judgment with your servant, 3 For the enemy pursues my soul. 4 Therefore my spirit is overwhelmed within me. 5 I remember the days of old. 6 I spread out my hands to you. 7 Hurry to answer me, Yahweh. 8 Cause me to hear your loving kindness in the morning, 9 Deliver me, Yahweh, from my enemies. 10 Teach me to do your will, 11 Revive me, Yahweh, for your name’s sake. 12 In your loving kindness, cut off my enemies,

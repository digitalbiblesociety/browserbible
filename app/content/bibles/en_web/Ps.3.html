3

1 Yahweh, how my adversaries have increased! 2 Many there are who say of my soul, 3 But you, Yahweh, are a shield around me, 4 I cry to Yahweh with my voice, 5 I laid myself down and slept. 6 I will not be afraid of tens of thousands of people 7 Arise, Yahweh! 8 Salvation belongs to Yahweh.

Psalm 34

33

1 Rejoice in Yahweh, you righteous! 2 Give thanks to Yahweh with the lyre. 3 Sing to him a new song. 4 For the word of Yahweh is right. 5 He loves righteousness and justice. 6 By Yahweh’s word, the heavens were made; 7 He gathers the waters of the sea together as a heap. 8 Let all the earth fear Yahweh. 9 For he spoke, and it was done. 10 Yahweh brings the counsel of the nations to nothing. 11 The counsel of Yahweh stands fast forever, 12 Blessed is the nation whose God is Yahweh, 13 Yahweh looks from heaven. 14 From the place of his habitation he looks out on all the inhabitants of the earth, 15 he who fashions all of their hearts; 16 There is no king saved by the multitude of an army. 17 A horse is a vain thing for safety, 18 Behold, Yahweh’s eye is on those who fear him, 19 to deliver their soul from death, 20 Our soul has waited for Yahweh. 21 For our heart rejoices in him, 22 Let your loving kindness be on us, Yahweh,

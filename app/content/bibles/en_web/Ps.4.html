4

1 Answer me when I call, God of my righteousness. 2 You sons of men, how long shall my glory be turned into dishonor? 3 But know that Yahweh has set apart for himself him who is godly: 4 Stand in awe, and don’t sin. 5 Offer the sacrifices of righteousness. 6 Many say, “Who will show us any good?” 7 You have put gladness in my heart, 8 In peace I will both lay myself down and sleep,

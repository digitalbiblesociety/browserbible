Psalm 6

5

1 Give ear to my words, Yahweh. 2 Listen to the voice of my cry, my King and my God; 3 Yahweh, in the morning you shall hear my voice. 4 For you are not a God who has pleasure in wickedness. 5 The arrogant shall not stand in your sight. 6 You will destroy those who speak lies. 7 But as for me, in the abundance of your loving kindness I will come into your house. 8 Lead me, Yahweh, in your righteousness because of my enemies. 9 For there is no faithfulness in their mouth. 10 Hold them guilty, God. 11 But let all those who take refuge in you rejoice, 12 For you will bless the righteous.

Psalm 53

52

1 Why do you boast of mischief, mighty man? 2 Your tongue plots destruction, 3 You love evil more than good, 4 You love all devouring words, 5 God will likewise destroy you forever. 6 The righteous also will see it, and fear, 7 “Behold, this is the man who didn’t make God his strength, 8 But as for me, I am like a green olive tree in God’s house. 9 I will give you thanks forever, because you have done it.

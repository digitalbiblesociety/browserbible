56

1 Be merciful to me, God, for man wants to swallow me up. 2 My enemies want to swallow me up all day long, 3 When I am afraid, 4 In God, I praise his word. 5 All day long they twist my words. 6 They conspire and lurk, 7 Shall they escape by iniquity? 8 You number my wanderings. 9 Then my enemies shall turn back in the day that I call. 10 In God, I will praise his word. 11 I have put my trust in God. 12 Your vows are on me, God. 13 For you have delivered my soul from death,

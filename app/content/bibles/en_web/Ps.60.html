Psalm 61

60

1 God, you have rejected us. 2 You have made the land tremble. 3 You have shown your people hard things. 4 You have given a banner to those who fear you, 5 So that your beloved may be delivered, 6 God has spoken from his sanctuary: 7 Gilead is mine, and Manasseh is mine. 8 Moab is my wash basin. 9 Who will bring me into the strong city? 10 Haven’t you, God, rejected us? 11 Give us help against the adversary, 12 Through God we shall do valiantly,

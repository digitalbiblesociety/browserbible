Psalm 64

63

1 God, you are my God. 2 So I have seen you in the sanctuary, 3 Because your loving kindness is better than life, 4 So I will bless you while I live. 5 My soul shall be satisfied as with the richest food. 6 when I remember you on my bed, 7 For you have been my help. 8 My soul stays close to you. 9 But those who seek my soul, to destroy it, 10 They shall be given over to the power of the sword. 11 But the king shall rejoice in God.

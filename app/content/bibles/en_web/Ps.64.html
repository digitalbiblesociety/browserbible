Psalm 65

64

1 Hear my voice, God, in my complaint. 2 Hide me from the conspiracy of the wicked, 3 who sharpen their tongue like a sword, 4 to shoot innocent men from ambushes. 5 They encourage themselves in evil plans. 6 They plot injustice, saying, “We have made a perfect plan!” 7 But God will shoot at them. 8 Their own tongues shall ruin them. 9 All mankind shall be afraid. 10 The righteous shall be glad in Yahweh,

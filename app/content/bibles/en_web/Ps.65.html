Psalm 66

65

1 Praise waits for you, God, in Zion. 2 You who hear prayer, 3 Sins overwhelmed me, 4 Blessed is one whom you choose, and cause to come near, 5 By awesome deeds of righteousness, you answer us, 6 Who by his power forms the mountains, 7 who stills the roaring of the seas, 8 They also who dwell in faraway places are afraid at your wonders. 9 You visit the earth, and water it. 10 You drench its furrows. 11 You crown the year with your bounty. 12 The wilderness grasslands overflow. 13 The pastures are covered with flocks.

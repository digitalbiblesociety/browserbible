Psalm 98

97

1 Yahweh reigns! 2 Clouds and darkness are around him. 3 A fire goes before him, 4 His lightning lights up the world. 5 The mountains melt like wax at the presence of Yahweh, 6 The heavens declare his righteousness. 7 Let all them be shamed who serve engraved images, 8 Zion heard and was glad. 9 For you, Yahweh, are most high above all the earth. 10 You who love Yahweh, hate evil. 11 Light is sown for the righteous, 12 Be glad in Yahweh, you righteous people!

Psalm 100

99

1 Yahweh reigns! Let the peoples tremble. 2 Yahweh is great in Zion. 3 Let them praise your great and awesome name. 4 The King’s strength also loves justice. 5 Exalt Yahweh our God. 6 Moses and Aaron were among his priests, 7 He spoke to them in the pillar of cloud. 8 You answered them, Yahweh our God. 9 Exalt Yahweh, our God.

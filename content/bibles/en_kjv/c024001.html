1 The words of Jeremiah the son of Hilkiah of the priests in Anathoth in the land of Benjamin 2 To whom the word came in the days of Josiah the son of Amon king of Judah in the thirteenth year of his reign 3 It came also in the days of Jehoiakim the son of Josiah king of Judah unto the end of the eleventh year of Zedekiah the son of Josiah king of Judah unto the carrying away of Jerusalem captive in the fifth month 4 Then the word came unto me, saying 5 Before I formed thee in the belly I knew thee; and before thou camest forth out of the womb I sanctified I ordained thee a prophet unto the nations 6 Then said I, Ah Lord GOD behold, I cannot speak a child 7 said unto me, Say a child for thou shalt go to all that I shall send thee, and whatsoever I command thee thou shalt speak 8 Be not afraid of their faces with thee to deliver thee, saith 9 put forth his hand and touched my mouth said unto me, Behold, I have put my words in thy mouth 10 See I have this day set thee over the nations and over the kingdoms to root out and to pull down and to destroy and to throw down to build and to plant 11 Moreover the word came unto me, saying Jeremiah what seest thou? And I said I see a rod of an almond tree 12 Then said unto me, Thou hast well seen for I will hasten my word to perform 13 And the word came unto me the second time saying What seest thou? And I said I see a seething pot and the face toward the north 14 said unto me, Out of the north an evil shall break forth upon all the inhabitants of the land 15 For, lo, I will call all the families of the kingdoms of the north saith and they shall come and they shall set every one his throne at the entering of the gates of Jerusalem and against all the walls thereof round about and against all the cities of Judah 16 And I will utter my judgments against them touching all their wickedness who have forsaken me, and have burned incense unto other gods and worshipped the works of their own hands 17 Thou therefore gird up thy loins and arise and speak unto them all that I command thee: be not dismayed at their faces lest I confound thee before 18 For, behold, I have made thee this day a defenced city and an iron pillar and brasen walls against the whole land against the kings of Judah against the princes thereof, against the priests thereof, and against the people of the land 19 And they shall fight against thee; but they shall not prevail with thee, saith to deliver

43

1 when Jeremiah had made an end of speaking unto all the people all the words their God their God had sent all these words 2 Then spake Azariah the son of Hoshaiah and Johanan the son of Kareah and all the proud men saying unto Jeremiah Thou speakest falsely our God hath not sent thee to say Go not into Egypt to sojourn 3 But Baruch the son of Neriah setteth thee on against us, for to deliver us into the hand of the Chaldeans that they might put us to death and carry us away captives into Babylon 4 So Johanan the son of Kareah and all the captains of the forces and all the people obeyed not the voice to dwell in the land of Judah 5 But Johanan the son of Kareah and all the captains of the forces took all the remnant of Judah that were returned from all nations whither they had been driven to dwell in the land of Judah 6 men and women and children and the king' daughters and every person that Nebuzaradan the captain of the guard had left with Gedaliah the son of Ahikam the son of Shaphan and Jeremiah the prophet and Baruch the son of Neriah 7 So they came into the land of Egypt for they obeyed not the voice thus came to Tahpanhes 8 Then came the word unto Jeremiah in Tahpanhes saying 9 Take great stones in thine hand and hide them in the clay in the brickkiln at the entry of Pharaoh' house in Tahpanhes in the sight of the men of Judah 10 And say unto them, Thus saith of hosts the God of Israel Behold, I will send and take Nebuchadrezzar the king of Babylon my servant and will set his throne upon these stones that I have hid and he shall spread his royal pavilion 11 And when he cometh he shall smite the land of Egypt for death to death for captivity to captivity for the sword to the sword 12 And I will kindle a fire in the houses of the gods of Egypt and he shall burn them, and carry them away captives and he shall array himself with the land of Egypt as a shepherd putteth on his garment and he shall go forth from thence in peace 13 He shall break also the images of Bethshemesh in the land of Egypt and the houses of the gods of the Egyptians shall he burn with fire
